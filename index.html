What we do

The GPUs that trained the current generation of AI are leaving the datacenter. vonArgo gives that hardware a second working life — as quiet, self-contained inference machines that plug into an ordinary workstation.

We source decommissioned NVIDIA HGX-class GPU systems with documented provenance, then re-engineer everything around them: power delivery, liquid cooling, and the PCIe fabric that connects four NVLinked data-center GPUs to a single slot in your machine.

How a unit is built

1 — SOURCE

Verified salvage

HGX baseboards and SXM modules from US datacenter decommissions. Documented origin, inspected on arrival.

2 — INTEGRATE

Re-engineering

54 V power distribution, copper cold plates and a closed liquid loop, PCIe Gen4 switch fabric to the host.

3 — DELIVER

Burn-in & documentation

Every unit will ship after extended load testing, with its own build record and burn-in report.
